Drone Usability Research

The x Drone is an entry level drone that is aimed towards a younger
audience or beginners to such technology.

Containing basic but necessary features, this product is able to deliver fundemental
experience, that forms core learning for more advanced drones.

It also provides enjoyment for its users. Whether you are young, old, a pro or
a beginner, the x can provide a good amount of enterntainment.





The purpose and the contents of this website is to provide an insight on the x drone.
How well is the product designed?
From hardware to software, ergonomics and visuals,
a qualitative research method had been done to provide accurate information on the product.
